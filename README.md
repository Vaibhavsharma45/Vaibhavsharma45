I am a final-year BCA student and aspiring Data Scientist with hands-on experience in data analysis, dashboard development, and business intelligence. My 6-month internship as a Data Analyst gave me practical exposure to the full analytics pipeline — from raw data ingestion and cleaning to delivering KPI dashboards and actionable insights for stakeholders.

## Internship Experience

**Data Analyst Intern** | 6 Months

During my internship, I worked within a data team responsible for delivering analytical outputs to business stakeholders. Key contributions included:

- **Data Preparation:** Cleaned and preprocessed datasets to ensure quality and consistency before analysis. Handled missing values, outlier detection, and data type standardization across multiple sources.
- **Dashboard Development:** Designed and maintained KPI dashboards in Power BI and Tableau, enabling stakeholders to track performance metrics and identify trends at a glance.
- **SQL Reporting:** Wrote SQL queries to extract, join, and aggregate data from relational databases for recurring business reports and ad-hoc requests.
- **Statistical Analysis:** Conducted exploratory data analysis (EDA) and trend discovery to surface insights that informed operational decisions.
- **Business Communication:** Translated analytical findings into clear summaries for non-technical audiences, directly supporting data-driven decision-making.
